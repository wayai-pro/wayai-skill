# Billing FAQ

## How am I charged?
Subscriptions renew on the same day each month (or year). Charges appear on your statement as `ACME CO`.

## How do I change plans?
Self-serve in **Settings → Billing**. Upgrades are immediate; downgrades take effect at the end of the current period.

## Where do I find my invoices?
**Settings → Billing → Invoice history**. PDF download is available for each invoice.

## My card was declined — what now?
Update the card in **Settings → Billing → Payment method**. We retry failed charges twice over 5 days before suspending service. No data is lost during suspension; access is restored when payment succeeds.

## Tax / VAT
We collect tax based on the billing address. To add a VAT ID, go to **Settings → Billing → Tax info**.
